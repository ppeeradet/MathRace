📖 How to Play

Controls

➡️ Press the Left/Right arrow keys, or tap the ◀ ▶ buttons on the screen to switch between the left, middle, and right lanes.

Mission

The top of the screen shows your mission, like "Collect equations where the result = 12" or "Collect only Multiplication" — watch for the equation pods floating down and fly your ship into the one that matches the mission!

Lives & Score

✅ Correct catch: +10 points, your ship speeds up, sparkles fly, and you'll hear the equation read aloud
❌ Wrong catch: lose 1 life (you start with 3), the screen shakes, and it explodes!
💥 Out of lives = Game Over

Tips

Read the equation quickly and decide before the pod reaches your ship. The more you catch correctly, the faster you'll go. Complete missions for bonus points!
